## The Partnership

Jot.digital is a Calgary-based technology consulting firm specialising in app modernisation, cloud migration, Microsoft ERP, and operational technology. Through an ongoing partnership, I provide senior technical leadership on complex engagements—serving as fractional CTO, lead architect, or technical advisor depending on client needs.

This model lets Jot.digital take on sophisticated digital transformation projects with CTO-level expertise while maintaining the flexibility of a boutique consulting firm.
